Stammer Minecraft Club

Gaming club for young people to stammer out loud and proud!

Our club is a fun, supportive and creative community for young people who stammer to talk, play, build and meet other young people who stammer.
Our members are encouraged to say what they want to say authentically, however the words flow out.
We want to hear your voice! Join the team design and build challenges, talk with your team, stammer out loud and be proud of who you are, exactly as you are.
