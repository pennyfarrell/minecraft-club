Stammer Minecraft Club

Gaming club for young people to stammer out loud and proud!

Our club is a fun, supportive and creative community for young people who stammer to place to talk, play, build and be authentic.
Our members can say what they want to say, whether they stammer or not.
We want to hear your voice and encourage you to talk with us, stammer out loud and be proud of who you are, exactly as you are.
