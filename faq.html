Frequently Asked Questions
by parents

How much does it costs to join the Minecraft Stammer club?
The Minecraft Stammer Club is free to join and free to attend each gaming session.
Will they do any speech therapy in the game session?
No, the Minecraft Stammer Club is a social activity for young people who stammer to speak freely, stammer freely and make new friends in the stammering community.
Is the club supervised?
Yes! We have youth officer with each small group. The group play in a private world called a Minecraft Realm which has invite-only access. Our Youth Officers will supervise the game play and chat to make sure everyone is having fun and can join in the chat and team activities.
What equipment do we need?
You will need Minecraft Bedrock edition. You can join our Realm from any device - PC, tablet, XBox*, PS4*, PS5*, NS Switch*, iPad). We will voice chat on Zoom during the session - invites will be emailed when you register for the event. *You also need an online game pass to join from these consoles.


Frequently Asked Questions
by kids

What will we do in the game session?
We play Minecraft Bedrock in Realms. We play in creative-peaceful mode to build new creations and expand our private world. While we play Minecraft, we will use Zoom for the voice chat. You can join in with the chat as much or as little as you like. If you are a pro Minecrafter that's awesome and you'll be able to show off your skills. If this is a new game for you, we'd love to show you how creative mode works and teach you some cool new building skills!
I'm not sure about meeting new people and talking to them. Do I have to talk in the session?
No, the Minecraft Stammer Club is a social activity for young people who stammer to speak freely, stammer freely and make new friends in the stammering community. We think it's really helpful for young people to hear other people stammer, so it can be really good to listen in to the chat until you get to know your new friends. We want you to have fun and feel comfortable! We think it is ok to stammer so you can talk to us freely, whatever way the words travel out
I am not pro, can I still join in?
Yes of course! We have a mix of game experience in our group. If you are a noob, we'll happily show you the ropes and you'll soon get your imagination going and your creative groove will kick in! If you are a pro, please show us your building moves so we can make some fabulous team creations!
What game settings do you play with?
We play in creative and peaceful mode with no daylight cycle. This means we can let our imagination run free and build amazing things in our world! It's easier to build in sunlight without worrying about mobs!
